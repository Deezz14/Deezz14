Requirements

We are looking for passionate and talented individuals to join our team. To be considered for a developer position at Hot Beans Web, you should meet the following requirements:

  • Proven experience as a web developer or similar role
  • Strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Vue, Angular)
  • Understanding of server-side languages (e.g., Node.js, Python, PHP)
  • Knowledge of database systems (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB)
  • Version control using Git
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Continuously staying updated with industry trends and technologies

If you possess these skills and are eager to contribute to our innovative projects, we encourage you to apply and become a valuable member of our web development team.
